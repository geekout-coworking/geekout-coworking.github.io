Welcome to Geekout.

Geekout is fantabulous co-working space where you can work and collaborate on their personal or community projects. We started our first launchpad at Lokhandwala Andheri which was the only 24hr operating co-working space with an in-house mini kitchen and unlimited coffee to boost your outputs spirits. We have also hosted hackathons and geekcamps at the respective venues.

We got a beautiful space for anyone in and around Andheri West area, Its 2 min from Lokhandwala area, 15 mins from DN Nagar and Versova metro. Rates are as low as 3000 INR to 12000 INR with a well-stocked pantry and fridge. Come to visit us anytime during the day. We are open 24H.

Geekout just doesn't provide space to work but to grow as well. Its the only coworking place in Mumbai to provide:

  • Talent pool connect to any technology requirement of a start up.
  • Connect with Tech Consultants to solve any technological issues you face in your projects or startups.
  • A "special freelancer plan" where we provide 10+ leads every week( avg. worth of 4000 USD per lead) to co working freelancers.
  • Special discount for hackathons and workshops organised by Codeniti and CodeGurukul.
  • Goodies like Free AWS Credits, Free Github Repos, Free Coffee and so on are just added perks at our launchpad.

Plans and Pricing

  • Virtual Desk for your startup at Geekout

  • Free Coffee / Tea
